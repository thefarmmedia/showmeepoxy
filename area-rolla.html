Our Process — Rolla

How a Show Me Epoxy Install Works

The same proven process on every Rolla project — because skipping steps is how floors fail.

1

Free Consultation

We assess your concrete and give you a real, itemized price — no vague ranges.

2

Diamond Grinding

Every slab gets mechanically ground to open the pores and ensure a permanent bond.

3

Crack Repair

All cracks, divots, and spalling are repaired before any coating is applied.

4

Base Coat

Industrial-grade epoxy base coat applied for maximum adhesion and durability.

5

Topcoat & Cure

Polyaspartic or polyurethane topcoat seals everything — walk in 24 hrs.
