Our Process

How a Show Me Epoxy Install Works

Every install follows the same proven process — because skipping steps is how floors fail.

1

Free Consultation & Quote

We assess your concrete, discuss your goals, and give you a real, no-pressure quote before any commitment.

2

Diamond Grinding & Surface Prep

We mechanically grind your slab to open the pores — the step most installers skip and why their floors eventually fail.

3

Crack Repair & Leveling

Any cracks, divots, or imperfections are repaired before coating so your finished floor is smooth and seamless.

4

Epoxy or Polyaspartic Coating

Industrial-grade base coat applied for maximum bond, then your chosen color and flake system laid in full broadcast.

5

Topcoat & Cure

A durable polyurethane or polyaspartic topcoat seals everything — giving you a surface that resists scratches, chemicals, and UV.
