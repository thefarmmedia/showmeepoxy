How much does basement epoxy floors cost in Rolla, MO?

Pricing depends on square footage, concrete condition, repairs, coating type, and finish. A quick quote is the most accurate way to price the project.

Do you grind the concrete first?

Yes. Mechanical diamond grinding is part of our prep-first process because adhesion is what keeps a floor from peeling.

Can you repair cracks before coating?

Yes. Cracks, pits, and damaged spots are addressed before the coating system is installed.
