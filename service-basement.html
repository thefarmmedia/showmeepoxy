Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my basement floor suitable for epoxy?

Most basement floors are great candidates. The main consideration is moisture — we test every slab before quoting and recommend the right system for your specific conditions.

What if my basement has moisture issues?

We use moisture-mitigating primers and moisture-tolerant epoxy formulations for slabs with high vapor emission. We'll identify this before installation and factor it into your quote.

How long does basement epoxy installation take?

Most basement projects are completed in 1–2 days depending on size, prep needed, and system chosen. We'll give you a specific timeline during your free consultation.
